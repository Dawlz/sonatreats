About SonaTreats

Hi, I am Aderonke, an alumna of University of Ilorin (BSc Biochemistry and MBA - In View) and the CEO of SonaTreats.
SonaTreats is a confectionary outfit that started out as an hobby and grew into a brand that is still growing.
We are focused on satisfying the cravings and needs of our clients with yummy treats made with the finest quality of ingredients.
Explore the options we have, place your orders and let us treat your taste buds to the best value for your money!
Our core values are Growth and Value!
